Good to know

Questions, answered

What ages is it for?

Best for ages 3–9. Read-alongs serve pre-readers and new readers; longer early-reader titles suit older kids.

What makes this different from Epic! or Amazon Kids+?

We're not trying to be a warehouse of 40,000 books. We're a small, curated shelf with a point of view — affirming, social-emotional, brave-girl and curiosity stories — each with a read-along and a printable. Quality and values over volume.

Can I cancel or pause anytime?

Yes — pause or cancel in two taps from your account, no questions and no guilt trips.

Do the books really come with read-alongs and activities?

Every title ships with narrated audio and at least one printable activity. That's the whole promise — never just a folder of PDFs.
