How We Make Money

Simple version first: we keep the lights on through paid library memberships and occasional affiliate links. Nothing we sell changes which books we publish or how we write about them.

The short version: families pay for the library, and sometimes we earn a small commission when readers buy a related book on Amazon or another store. Same price for you; it helps keep weekly stories free for members.

1. Library memberships

  • Monthly: $1.99/month for read-alongs, printables, and new weekly titles.
  • Annual: $19.99/year, which is the same as two months free.
  • Memberships pay for writing, narration, activity creation, and platform costs.

2. Affiliate links

  • Some pages include links to Amazon or other retailers.
  • If you buy after clicking, we may earn a small referral fee.
  • You pay the same price either way.
  • We disclose affiliate links on the page and in relevant emails.

3. What we don’t do

  • We don’t charge publishers to be featured.
  • We don’t accept paid placements disguised as recommendations.
  • We don’t let advertising or affiliate revenue change our story choices.
