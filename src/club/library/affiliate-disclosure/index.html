Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: July 2026

Bottom line up front: Some links on this site are affiliate links. If you click one and buy something, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. That helps keep the club free and the stories coming.

What this means

Made Out of Clay participates in affiliate programs including the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program. When you click an affiliate link and complete a qualifying purchase, the retailer pays us a small referral fee.

  • You pay the same price whether you use our links or go directly to the retailer.
  • We only link to books and products we would recommend regardless of commission.
  • Affiliate revenue is one way we support the free Digital Book Club and new weekly titles.

How we disclose

  • Every affiliate link is labeled or contextually clear.
  • Every newsletter or email that includes affiliate links also includes a disclosure notice.
  • This page is linked in the site footer from Made Out of Clay Library.

Questions

If you want to know whether a specific link is affiliate, or how the program works, email hello@madeoutofclayprod.com and we’ll answer directly.
