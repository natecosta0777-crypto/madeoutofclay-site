Help & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Isaiah Hartwell?
Isaiah Hartwell is the pen name for the author behind Made Out of Clay Productions. The imprint was built around real family experience — including a son named Clayton, who inspired the Just the Way You Are series. The name "Made Out of Clay" is a nod to him, and to the idea that all children are still becoming.
How many books are available right now?
There are currently 9 books across three series: At Sunny's Table (3 books), Just the Way You Are (3 books), and The Kingdom of Judea (3 books). All 9 are available on Amazon in both print and Kindle ebook formats.
What age range are the books for?
The books are picture books designed for ages 3–8, with content that holds up well for family read-aloud through age 10 and beyond. The Kingdom of Judea series tends to engage slightly older readers due to its adventure format and longer story arcs.
Are the books available in ebook format?
Yes — all 9 books are available in both print (premium color, 8.5×8.5 square) and Kindle ebook on Amazon. Print editions are particularly recommended for the illustration-heavy picture book format.
What is the Just the Way You Are series about?
Just the Way You Are is a neurodiversity and inclusion series centered on Clayton — a child with ADHD and autism, and the real inspiration behind the imprint's name. The books are written in identity-first language, each includes a "Note to Grown-Ups" with practical guidance for caregivers, and every book in this series has been through a sensitivity review process.
Is the Kingdom of Judea series religious?
The series is faith-infused but not explicitly denominational. The values — loyalty, courage, faithfulness, protecting what you love — are woven into the story through character and choice rather than direct instruction. Families who want faith-forward books will find what they're looking for; families from different traditions will find a well-crafted adventure story. Both readings work.
Do I need to read the books in order?
Within each series, reading in order enriches the experience — the characters develop across the three books and some emotional payoffs land better if you've followed the arc. That said, each book is written to stand on its own. Starting anywhere in a series won't leave you lost.
Can I use these books in a classroom or library?
Absolutely. The Just the Way You Are and At Sunny's Table series in particular work well in classroom read-aloud settings and school library collections. Each book comes with content that supports discussion — the "Note to Grown-Ups" in the JTWYA series is especially useful for educators. For bulk or classroom orders, contact us through the contact page.
Will there be more books?
Yes. New titles are in development across all three series. The best way to hear about new releases is to join the mailing list — we announce new books there first.
How do I get in touch?
Use the contact page for press inquiries, school/library orders, or general questions. We try to respond within 2–3 business days.
