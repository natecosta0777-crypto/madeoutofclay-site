### Email 2 — Story behind the series (sent Day 3)

**Subject:** The child who started it all

There's a boy named Clayton.

He has ADHD and autism, and for years the books available to him either treated his differences as problems to solve — or as inspiration fodder for the neurotypical kids around him.

Neither felt right. Neither felt like *him*.

So we built a series around him. Not about him in the distant, clinical way — but from inside his experience. What does it feel like to have a mind that moves at that speed? What is it like to need it quiet when the world won't cooperate? What does it take to try something genuinely hard?

Those are the questions the **Just the Way You Are** series answers. Three books. Identity-first language throughout. A "Note to Grown-Ups" in each one with practical guidance, not platitudes. All sensitivity reviewed.

And a back page in every book that says: *About Clayton.* Because he's real, and his story is worth knowing.

→ [Explore the Just the Way You Are series](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Isaiah+Hartwell+Just+the+Way+You+Are)

If you know a child like Clayton — or if you *are* a grown-up who was once a child like Clayton — we hope these books find you.

Isaiah Hartwell
Made Out of Clay Productions
